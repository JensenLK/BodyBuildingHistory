Introduction to Bodybuilding

Bodybuilding has a long history dating back to the late 19th century. Its origins are deeply rooted in physical culture movements, emphasizing strength, aesthetics, and health. As bodybuilding evolved and grew in popularity the focus of the sport changed. This included several shifts from aesthetics to "mass monsters" and back again.
